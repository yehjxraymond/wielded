Standard Token

A clean implementation of the ERC20 token.

Without fanciful code, this token contract leaves no room for bugs. The standard token is suitable for tokenization of different assets on the Ethereum network. Tokens minted from this contract will be sent to the creator's wallet.

Recommended token contract for most use-cases.

*Token sale contract is not included
